Who We Are

The Aeronautical Academy of Ananda College is one of the college’s respected student societies, recognized for its contribution to aviation education within and beyond the school community.

Through lectures, workshops, field visits and hands-on projects, the academy provides students with opportunities to understand aviation fundamentals, explore future career paths and develop the confidence required to lead and innovate.

Our mission is to inspire young aviation enthusiasts and guide them towards becoming future professionals who can contribute to Sri Lanka’s aviation sector and the global aerospace community.
