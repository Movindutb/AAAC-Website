Throughout the year 2025, we have been engaged in various projects and functions aimed at enlightening students about aviation, not only within our school but also beyond our alma mater.

These initiatives have provided valuable opportunities for young aviation enthusiasts to expand their knowledge, develop practical skills, and engage in the wider field of aeronautics.

Through each project, the Aeronautical Academy has continued to promote curiosity, teamwork, and passion for aviation while strengthening its role as a leading student society dedicated to inspiring future aviators.
